Ready for more

MASCOT Circuit Zandvoort is known as a true 'Old School' circuit, with fast and challenging curves formed by the natural dunes. With a track length of 4.3 kilometers, Circuit Zandvoort is one of the shorter circuits on the F1 calendar. Hardly anywhere else do drivers race past the fans so often!
